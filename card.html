Cow

Cow is the holy animal in India. It is worshipped in many parts of India.

  • Scientific Name: Bos taurus
  • Average Length: 100 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands

    • They are a prominent modern member of the subfamily Bovinae, are the most widespread species of the genus Bos, and are most commonly classified collectivelyas Bos taurus.
